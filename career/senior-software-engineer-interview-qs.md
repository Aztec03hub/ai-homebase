Senior Software Engineer Interview

Application

I applied online. The process took 2 weeks.I interviewed at Luxury Presence (New York, NY) in Feb 2025

Interview

The interview process starts with a quick HR screening to align expectations, followed by two technical interviews focused on practical skills. Then, there's a system design interview to assess architectural thinking, and finally, a cultural fit interview with the team.

Interview questions [1]

Question 1

They asked me to implement a TypeScript class named Firewall, with two methods: addIp() to register allowed IPs and isAllowed() to check if a given IP is authorized based on the previously added entries.
